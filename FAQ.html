FAQ

These are some questions you may have about our password checker, password attacks, or secure passwords.
A secure password has:
  • 10+ characters (15+ best)
  • Letters (Uppercase and lowercase)
  • Numbers
  • Special characters
  • A variety of characters
Try to avoid using dictionary words in a password.
This Password Safety Manager can help you decide on a secure password, see if your password is secure, and help you learn more about secure passwords and other related topics. This will all benefit you so your password is safe and more unlikely to be hacked. You can use the Password Security Checker to check to see if your password is secure, or use the Password Generator to generate a secure password.
A secure password is important because it makes it harder for hackers to hack your account. If they do, they can access and edit your information, delete your account, or even change your password so you can't access it, among other things.
